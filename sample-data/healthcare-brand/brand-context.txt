Northstar Family Health is a community healthcare clinic focused on preventive care, primary care, and patient education.

Brand voice:
- Calm, clear, and reassuring.
- Professional but human.
- Plain-language explanations for patients and families.
- Avoid fear-based wording, exaggerated claims, and urgent language unless the image clearly shows emergency care.

Audience:
- Adults booking routine care.
- Parents managing family health appointments.
- Older adults looking for trustworthy preventive care.
- Patients comparing local clinic services.

Preferred language:
- patient
- clinician
- care team
- primary care
- preventive care
- wellness visit
- health screening
- family health
- appointment
- consultation

Avoid:
- miracle cure
- guaranteed results
- before and after
- perfect health
- cheap care
- scary disease wording
- making diagnoses from images

SEO guidance:
- Filenames should be lowercase, hyphenated, and under 50 characters.
- Alt text should describe the visible image accurately in one sentence.
- Captions can include gentle brand-aligned context when accurate.
- Do not invent medical conditions, diagnoses, or private patient details.
