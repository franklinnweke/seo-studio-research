# Contributors and provenance

SEO Studio began as a PROG8751 capstone team project and was later extended into a publication-oriented research system. The public research repository preserves the original commit authors, timestamps, and application history.

## Original capstone team

- Ayobami Aladenoye — frontend development and quality assurance.
- Michael Abelola — backend and database development.
- Franklin Nweke — documentation, database work, and quality assurance.

## Research edition

Franklin Nweke leads and maintains the research extension, including the context-aware metadata architecture, evaluation harness, DGX/Ollama experimental workflow, evidence management, calibration analysis, and working publication materials.

The capstone team approved republication of the shared application foundation in `franklinnweke/seo-studio-research`. Detailed technical attribution remains available in the Git history.

Software contribution credit does not by itself determine authorship of a resulting paper. Publication authorship and CRediT roles will be agreed and recorded separately before submission.
